Teaching and Learning process

Teaching is an art of stealing one hour from students without bringing it to their notice. It involves laying a strong foundation of basics and bringing awareness about the latest industrial trends. Just job seekers are not going to improve our GDP. Igniting the potential of Enterpreneurship and inspiring them to be job providers is the need of the hour. We do not know what jobs they do, the jobs which they may do might not be created yet. A teacher should prepare the students for the uncertainty of life. the only way they can handle it is by becoming continous learners.

I would like to teach my course in such a way that they embrace reasearch culture to find the truth/fact and inculcate lifelong learning.
