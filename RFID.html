RFID

In all the things of the digital world, there is always something to be communicated. Integrating the physical world and virtual by connecting the sensors to be a network is the rationable behind IOT. The 'things' should be uniquely identifiable. The need of Automatic Identification and Data Capture is resolved by using Radio Frequency Identification(RFID)


RFID: Enabler of IOT

Introduction:


The RFID is a Dedicated Short Range Communication (DSRC) fundamentally used for tracking with less/no human interverntion. The non contact identification (Communication ranged from 10cm upto several m) of high speed moving objects with simultaneous recognition of multiple tags makes it a quick and convenient option for IOT applications. Long-life, water proof, anti-magnetic and low cost add to its advantages.
