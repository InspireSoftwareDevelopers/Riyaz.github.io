Philosophy of Life

GOD

God is not meant for weak, for God is Great and Great things can be attained only with enormous strength & courage.

LIFE

There are many things to achieve, many things to feel, many things to learn but only one life...

SUCCESS

Be loud and clear while asking what you want from the universe;
you will get it...

LEADERSHIP

Follow the rules,
command will come by itself.

RELIGION

Kindness
is my religion.

PROFESSIONALISM

The moment I insult my colleague, I loose respect of my fellow colleagues..

TECHNOLOGY

Many people think that Artificial intelligence will take over humans, little do they know that already we are under the control of machines...

RELATIONSHIP

If your focus is on how you can contribute in a relationship without any demand and complaints, you will set a great example.

LOVE

The whole world is based on love, for love is faith in God, love is respect to father, love is freedom of expression to mother, love is care to delicate things, and love is sacrifice to things we are responsible.
